FAQ
Common Questions
How do I book a ticket? +
Select your route, date, and seat class using our booking form above. Choose your departure time, complete payment via M-Pesa or Bank Transfer, and your e-ticket will be generated instantly.
Can I cancel or change my booking? +
Yes. Cancellations made 24+ hours before departure receive a full refund minus a 5% processing fee. Changes are free if made more than 6 hours before departure. Contact us at info@rayan.co.ke.
How does parcel delivery work? +
Fill in the sender and receiver details using our parcel form. Drop off your parcel at the nearest Rayan Coach terminal. Your parcel travels on the next available bus to the destination city. Same-day or next-day delivery options available.
Is there a loyalty program? +
Yes! Every trip earns you Rayan Points. 100 points = KES 100 off your next booking. Gold members (500+ trips) get VIP upgrades, priority boarding, and exclusive discounts.
What if my bus is delayed? +
You'll receive SMS and app notifications for any delays. Use our live tracking to see the real-time location of your bus. For delays over 2 hours, you're eligible for a partial refund or a complimentary meal voucher.
