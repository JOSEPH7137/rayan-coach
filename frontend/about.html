About Rayan Coach

Kenya's Premier Intercity Transport Provider

Our Story

Rayan Coach Limited was founded with a vision to revolutionize intercity travel in Kenya. Since our inception, we have been committed to providing travelers with the comfort and convenience they deserve. Our journey began with a single bus and a dream to make travel across Kenya safe, comfortable, and reliable.

Our Mission

To deliver premium intercity transport services that exceed customer expectations through innovation, safety, and unwavering commitment to excellence.

Our Vision

To be Kenya's most trusted and preferred transport provider, setting the standard for comfort, safety, and reliability in the region.
